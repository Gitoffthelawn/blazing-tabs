Blazing Tabs

Google Chrome: How to up a hotkey for Blazing Tabs

  1. Go to Settings -> "Extensions"
  2. Click the Menu Button and select "Keyboard shortcuts"
  3. Find the section for "Blazing Tabs" and configure e.g. Ctrl+E as the hotkey.

Firefox: How to set up a hotkey for Blazing Tabs

  1. Go to Settings -> "Extensions & Themes"
  2. Click the Cogwheel and select "Manage Extension Shortcuts"
  3. Find the section for "Blazing Tabs" and configure e.g. Ctrl+E as the hotkey.
